Subtraction of Natural Numbers

Prerequisites

  • Natural Numbers
  • Comparison of Natural Numbers
  • Addition of Natural Numbers

Informal description

When \(b\) objects are removed from a group of \(a\) objects, the resulting group's size is defined as the difference of \(a\) and \(b\), also known as \(a\) subtracted by \(b\), denoted as \(a-b\). Taking the difference of two numbers is known as subtraction.
