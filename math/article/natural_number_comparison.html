Informal description

If a group of size \(a\) is larger than a group of size \(b\), \(a\) is said to be greater than \(b\), denoted \(a > b\). Similarly, \(a\) being less than \(b\) is denoted as \(a < b\).

Besides greater than and less than, there are also symbols for greater than or equal to and less than or equal to, denoted \(\ge\) and \(\le\), respectively.
