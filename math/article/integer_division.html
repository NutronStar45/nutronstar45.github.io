Division of Integers

Informal description

Prerequisites

  • Integers
  • Division of Natural Numbers

Division can be defined through multiplication. For natural numbers \(a\) and \(b\) with \(a\) divisible by \(b\), we have

\[ (a/b) \times b = a \]

Integer division can be defined by generalizing this equation to integers \(a\) and \(b\).
