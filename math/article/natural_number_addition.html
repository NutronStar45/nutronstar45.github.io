Addition of Natural Numbers

Prerequisites

  • Natural Numbers

Informal description

When two groups of things are combined into one, the final group's size can be determined from the sizes of the individual groups. If the sizes of the groups are \(a\) and \(b\), the size of the final group is defined as the sum of \(a\) and \(b\), denoted as \(a+b\). Taking the sum of two numbers is known as addition.
